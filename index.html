Frequently Asked Questions About Latvia (FAQ)

Q:

Where is Latvia located?

A:
In the Baltic region of Northern Europe next to the Baltic Sea between Lithuania and Estonia
Q:

What is the capital of Latvia?

A:
Riga
Q:

What is the official language of Latvia?

A:
Latvian
Q:

What is the currency of Latvia?

A:
Euro (€) (EUR)
Q:

When did Latvia join the European Union (EU)?

A:
1 May 2004
Q:

When did Latvia join NATO?

A:
29 March 2004
Q:

When did Latvia declare independence from USSR?

A:
21 August 1991
